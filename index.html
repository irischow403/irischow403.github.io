Monthly Sales Performance

Explanation

This line chart shows the monthly sales performance over the past year. The data reveals a clear upward trend, with notable peaks during the holiday season (November and December). The steady growth indicates successful business expansion and increased customer engagement throughout the year.

Product Category Distribution

Explanation

This pie chart illustrates the distribution of sales across different product categories. Electronics dominate with 35% of total sales, followed by Clothing at 25%, Home & Garden at 20%, Books at 12%, and Sports at 8%. This distribution helps identify which product categories are most popular and where to focus inventory and marketing efforts.

Customer Satisfaction Ratings

Explanation

This bar chart displays customer satisfaction ratings across different service aspects. Product Quality leads with a score of 4.5 out of 5, while Shipping Speed scores 4.2, Customer Service scores 4.3, and Value for Money scores 4.0. These metrics help identify strengths and areas for improvement in the customer experience.
